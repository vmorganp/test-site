# Apple Butter

Source: Family

## Ingredients
[[UnitConversions | Unit Conversions]]

- [ ] 6 qts apples
- [ ] 1 cup water
      OR
- [ ] 1 gallon applesauce
- [ ] little water

AND

- [ ] 4 cups sugar
- [ ] 3/4 tsp cloves
- [ ] 3 tbsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1.5 tsp nutmeg
- [ ] 3/4 tsp ginger

## Instructions

1. Blend well
2. cook on low heat with cover or wire-meshe until thick
3. Jar
4. sterilize EVERYTHING and use a new flat every time
