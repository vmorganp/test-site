Prep: 5 minutes
Servings: 8

# Ingredients
[[UnitConversions | Unit Conversions]]

- [ ] ⅓ Cup Olive Oil
- [ ] 3 tablespoon Apple Cider Vinegar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon Dijon Mustard
- [ ] 3 tablespoon Honey
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon Salt
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Ground Black Pepper

# Steps

1. Place all ingredients in jar
2. Close lid
3. shake until combined and then refrigerate
